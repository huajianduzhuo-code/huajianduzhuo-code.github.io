This paper provides a rigorous mathematical analysis of the concavity properties of consumption functions in economic models that incorporate habit formation. We establish theoretical conditions under which the consumption function maintains concavity.

**Authors**: Haoyu Liu, Lun Li

Published in *Journal of Mathematical Economics*, Volume 106, 2023, p. 102829.

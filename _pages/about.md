## Research Interests

My research focuses on the intersection of **machine learning** and **operations research**, with particular interests in:

- **Generative AI**: Diffusion Models, Large Language Models, Controllable Generative AI
- **Stochastic Modeling & Simulation**: Simulation, Inference Efficiency
- **Economics**: Economic Theory, A/B Testing & Experiment Design

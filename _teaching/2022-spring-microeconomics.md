## Course Description

This course covers classical microeconomic theory and recent developments, including:

- **Price Theory**: Consumer choice, producer theory, and market equilibrium analysis
- **Market Failure**: Externalities and public goods
- **Game Theory**: Strategic interactions and equilibrium concepts
- **Information Economics**: Markets with asymmetric information
